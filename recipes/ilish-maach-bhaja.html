Ilish maach Bhaja

Description

Fried ilish maach (hilsa fish). My childhood meal <3

Very tasty and crispy. Watch out for the bones!

Ingredients

  • 2 ilish steaks
  • fourth tsp of turmeric
  • half tsp of salt
  • 2 green chilies
  • mustard oil for frying

Directions

  1. marinate ilish with salt and turmeric
  2. fry in mustard oil until each side is brown
  3. add green chilies after one side is brown
  4. drain oil and serve with rice

Imitation Shark Fin Soup

Description

Hong Kong Style imitation shark fin soup!

Street food gourmet ^^

Main ingredients

  • 1lb pork shoulder
  • 20g of dried shiitake and 10gram of dried wood ear mushrooms
  • 42g Chinese vermicelli
  • 1 tsp ginger, 3 garlic cloves
  • 80g bamboo shoots
  • 1 large egg beaten

Soup

  • 4 cups unsalted chicken stock
  • 2 cups shiitake mushroom stock
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp roasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp fine salt
  • 5g rock sugar or granulated sugar
  • 1/8 tsp ground white pepper
  • thickener: 5tbsp cornstarch + 1/3 cup cold water mixed thoroughly

Directions

  1. rehydrate dried mushrooms and vermicelli
  2. squeeze liquid out of shiitake mushrooms, remove stems, slice thinly, save liquid for later
  3. roughly chop wood ear mushrooms
  4. cut rehydrated vermicelli to 5" long each
  5. cook pork shoulder until brown on all sides, set aside
  6. deglaze pot you browned the pork shoulder in with mushroom stock
  7. shred pork
  8. saute bamboo shoots, mushrooms, ginger, and cloves
  9. put all veggies in pressure cooker
  10. put all seasonings under Soup (except for thickener) in pressure cooker
  11. pressure cook this (keep pork aside still) for 18min
  12. turn off pressure cooker, throw in pork, thickener, and beaten egg while still hot, stir in a circle
  13. serve with drizzled sesame oil on top
